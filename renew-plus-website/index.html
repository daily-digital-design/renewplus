What we do

Support that meets you where you are

Every programme is built around you — your goals, your pace, your everyday life. Available through ACC or privately.

Return to Work Programmes

Back to work, at a pace that works.

We plan your return to work together — in steps, with your employer on board, so heading back feels doable, not daunting.

ACCPrivate

Workstation Assessments

A setup that works with your body.

We look at how you sit, move and work, then suggest simple changes that keep you comfortable all day, at the office or from home.

ACCPrivate

Concussion Services

Steady support while you recover.

Concussion can make everyday things feel hard. Together we provide practical strategies to manage symptoms, improve function and build confidence throughout recovery.

ACCPrivate

Training for Independence

Everyday skills, back in your hands.

From cooking and self-care to getting out and about, we build the daily skills and confidence to help you do more on your own.

Social Rehabilitation Assessments

About Renew Plus

Care from registered Occupational Therapists

Renew Plus is a New Zealand occupational therapy and rehabilitation practice built on a simple belief: with the right support, everyone can move toward the life they want to live.

Whether you're recovering from injury, returning to work, or rebuilding everyday independence, you'll be met with warmth, practical know-how and a plan that's truly your own.
